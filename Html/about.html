OUR STORY

START - 1995

Now many factories have been erected in Yangon, Mandalay and Sagaing for production and distribution across the Nationwide. Applying the high technology and using premium raw materials quality imported from Japan, Korea, Thailand and Thaiwan, we produce our Brands "ROYAL FAMILY" The Best, Dream and City Furniture
